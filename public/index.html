A Little About Me

I am a writer of nonfiction essays and articles living in San Francisco. Previously, I worked as an aviation finance lawyer, startup executive, and software developer. These eclectic vocations may not seem to have a lot in common, but they stem from some common desires: to articulate ideas clearly, to facilitate communication between people, and to explore new ways of thinking.

Topics I have been thinking and writing about lately include: technology, gender, motherhood, religion, secular ethics, psychedelic drugs, and the lost art of letter writing.
